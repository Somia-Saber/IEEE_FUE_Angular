Find Your Non-Technical Committee

HR Committee

Human resources Committee, responsible for all the "human resources" inside the branch, holding interviews, collecting members' data, organizing meetings, etc

   

PR Committee

Public relations Committee, responsible for all communication with entities outside the branch, they organize partnerships, find sponsors, write proposals, etc

   

Media Committee

Responsible for providing the social media pages with content including posts, designs and videos, they cover events and creativly market for the branch


   

Organization Committee

Responsible for planning and organizing events including all details and providing personnel to ensure the event goes as good as possible.
