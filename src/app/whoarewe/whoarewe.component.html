Who are we ?

  IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) is a global network of IEEE members and volunteers dedicated to promoting women engineers and scientists, and inspiring girls around the world to follow their academic interests in a career in engineering and science.

  IEEE-WIE Future University in Egypt Student Branch Affinity Group was established on January 2022, We aim to help our community, convey the message that math and science are fun, promote women engineers and scientists, empower and inspire girls to pursue their career in the field of engineering and technology.
